About Arihant Saini

My Journey

Guided by the Stars

I started Shambhava with a vision to demystify Jyotish Shastra and make authentic Vedic Astrology accessible to modern seekers. My readings focus on practical, actionable insights rather than fear-based predictions.

Through years of studying planetary transits, dasha systems, and ancient texts, I have helped thousands of individuals find clarity in their careers, relationships, and life's true purpose.

"Astrology is not about changing your destiny, but understanding your karma so you can walk your path with wisdom and grace."

Whether you're facing a challenging phase or simply seeking to align with your cosmic blueprint, my goal is to provide the guidance you need to make empowered decisions.
